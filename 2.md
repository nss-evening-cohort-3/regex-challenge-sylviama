#Exercise
Provide a regex that matches valid phone numbers with the forms 678-123-1122 and (678) 123-1122 while still allowing symbols to be optional. 


#Regex
-/(\(?)[0-9]{3}(\)|\-?)(\s?)[0-9]{3}(\-?)[0-9]{4}$/
-/(\(?)\d{3}(\)|\-?)(\s?)\d{3}(\-?)\d{4}$/
